Clean Water Access



Description

This challenge seeks solutions to improve access to clean drinking water in underserved communities, focusing on sustainability and local involvement.

Goals:

  • Develop low-cost water purification systems
  • Improve water distribution infrastructure
  • Educate communities on water conservation

Criteria for Submission:

  • Feasibility and impact
  • Community engagement
  • Environmental sustainability

Deadline:

April 15, 2025

Urgency Level:

High Urgency
