Renewable Energy for All



Description

This challenge aims to find innovative solutions that can provide renewable energy access to rural communities, ensuring sustainability and affordability.

Goals:

  • Identify local renewable resources
  • Create cost-effective energy solutions
  • Improve energy efficiency in rural areas

Criteria for Submission:

  • Adaptability to local contexts
  • Cost-effectiveness
  • Environmental sustainability

Deadline:

January 10, 2025

Urgency Level:

Medium Urgency
