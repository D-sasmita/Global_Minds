Solar-Powered Desalination

A sustainable solution to convert seawater into potable water using solar energy.

Solution Overview

The Solar-Powered Desalination system provides a clean, renewable energy solution to address the global water crisis by converting seawater into drinkable water using the power of the sun.

Impact

This solution aims to address the scarcity of clean drinking water, particularly in coastal regions with limited freshwater sources. The system is energy-efficient, environmentally friendly, and can be implemented in areas with high solar exposure.
