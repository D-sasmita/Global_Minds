Save the Oceans



Description

The health of our oceans is rapidly deteriorating due to pollution, climate change, and overfishing. This challenge seeks to gather innovative solutions to tackle ocean pollution and restore marine ecosystems.

Goals:

  • Reduce plastic waste in oceans
  • Develop technologies for cleaning up ocean pollution
  • Implement solutions to restore marine biodiversity

Criteria for Submission:

  • Innovative, scalable solutions
  • Feasibility of implementation
  • Potential impact on ocean ecosystems

Deadline:

December 31, 2024

Urgency Level:

High Urgency
Category
Climate Change
